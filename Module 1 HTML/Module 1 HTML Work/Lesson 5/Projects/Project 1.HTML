Making a Healthy Plate

Healthy Plate Gallery

Meals that are healthy contain a good amount (usually 3-5 each) of servings of grain, which gives carbohydrates and can be found in bread and rice, vegetables, which contain minerals and fibres and can be found in food like carrots, and cabbage, dairy, which gives calcium and can be found in milk and cheese, fruits, which give nutrients and can be found in foods like apples and bananas, and lastly lean meat and poultry, which can be found in foods like eggs and steak.

Nutritional Tips

Drink water

Water hydrates your body and it is more healthy compared to other drinks like soft drinks or coffee.

Consistently follow your diet.

Your path to eating healthy is pointless if you only eat a healthy plate once. A diet requires consistency and discipline. Get yourself motivated!

Quick Note

Make sure you are not buying processed foods because they can have unhealthy ingredients. Make sure to check the nutritional label, health star rating and ingredients list to confirm if an item is processed or not.
