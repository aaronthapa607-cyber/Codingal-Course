Glossary:

Math
The abstract science of numbers, quantity, space and structure.

Music
An art form that organises sounds, rhythms and tones into a coherent composition.

Art
A broad range of human activities and creative expressions that use skill and imagination to evoke a response and convey a concept.

Coding
The process of using programming languages to write step-by-step instructions that tell a computer or machine exactly what to do.

Free time
A period where you are not obligated to work, study or attend to other duties.

Pizza day
An event in school where pizza is handed out to students.

Movie
A visual work of art that uses a series of moving images and synchronised sound to tell a story or communicate an idea.
