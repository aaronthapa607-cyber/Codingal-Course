Planet Earth — Quick Guide

Our planet is full of incredible records, species, and facts. Here is a quick guide using lists!

Five Steps to Protect Our Planet:

  1. Switch off lights when you leave a room.
  2. Use a reusable water bottle every day.
  3. Walk or cycle instead of travelling by car.
  4. Plant a tree in your neighbourhood.
  5. Spread awareness — talk to your friends!

Incredible Natural Wonders:

  • Amazon Rainforest — home to 10% of all species on Earth
  • Great Barrier Reef — the largest coral reef, visible from space
  • Mount Everest — the highest point on Earth at 8,849 metres
  • Sahara Desert — the largest hot desert in the world

Key Terms:

Climate Change —
Long-term shifts in global temperature caused by human activity
Renewable Energy —
Energy from natural sources like sunlight and wind that never run out
Biodiversity —
The variety of all living species on Earth
